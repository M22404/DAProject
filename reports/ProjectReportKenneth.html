Motivation and Background¶

Give an overview of the project, motivation, background and goals.

Gambling in the modern era tend to have a negative connotation, with direct assotiation to terms like "addiction" and "wasting money". However, at the core of it, gambling is a form of entertainment that deals with chance and luck and in reality is not limited to adults. Gachapon machines in Japan for example is a form of "gambling" targeted at mostly children. The motivation of this project is to shed a light on the gambling industry as a form of entertainment and to understand how much impact it has on the economy; the goal of the project to analyze the gambling industry.

Methodology¶

You should demonstrate the data science life cycle here (from data acquisition to cleaning to EDA and analysis etc).

The methodology I used is as follows: search for data -> import data -> clean data -> EDA -> analysize EDA -> remove unneccesary EDA -> retry until acquired results.

To expand from here, I have searched the entire Internet for gambling data. Many datasets I could've acquired are locked behind a pay wall, until I hit The Transparency Project websites which provided a lot of raw datasets. I have downloaded many of them and in my initial testing, thought I needed any of them. However, as I progressed, I realise I don't understand the datasets and it's hard to join two of these datasets and end up abandoning almost all of them.

Importing the data is straightfoward. I faced a huge problem, which was I that I had to import and extra .sav and .sas7bdat files. It took me an entire week to solve this issue since the pyreadstat didn't want to cooperate with me.

Cleaning the data took some time. However, I mostly just removed some lines with unwanted NaNs and convert some columns from one datatype to another. For example, from String to DateTime or Float to Int32.

The EDA process was the hardest part since I had very little skill in using what I've learnt in practise. I ended up using mostly barcharts and some stripplots since I'm more comfortable with that.
